Software Engineer

Overview: Design and develop software applications. Requires problem-solving skills and knowledge of programming languages.

Skills Required: Programming, problem-solving, communication.

Educational Requirements: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.

Job Outlook: High demand with a growing number of opportunities.

Average Salary: $85,000 - $120,000 per year.

Industry Trends: Increasing focus on AI and machine learning.
